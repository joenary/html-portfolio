My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Interstellar

Interstellar is a visually stunning sci-fi movie that explores love, sacrifice, and the human spirit in the face of a dying Earth.

Inception

Inception is a cool movie where people go into dreams and do tricky stuff, I had to rewatch it a few times to enjoy it. :)

Monty Python

Monty Python movies are hilarious, with lots of silly jokes and absurd situations that'll make you laugh until your sides hurt.
